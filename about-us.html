About Us

Excellent Achievers Network International is a non-governmental and non-profit organisation saddled with a responsibility to ensure that teenagers and youth fulfil their God-given purpose on a wider scope. We fulfil the above aim through the following;
• Consultancy services and support to teenagers and youth in various areas of intervention
• Publications and distributions of intellectual and motivational literatures to target age groups
• Guidance and counselling via a counselling hub
• Sensitization of teenagers and youth for empowerment and mind reformation.


Our Mission is to:
• Promote the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals 3,4,&5
• To inspire the vulnerable children discover purpose, unleash and maximize their potentials.
Our Vision is to have society where every girl-child is seen as an integral member of the society and is given a level ground to attaining her full potentials required to transforming her world! .



OUR VISION

OUR MISSION

★ Humanitarian Services and sensitization outreaches to schools and communities; Sensitization and advocacy campaigns on the welfare of women and children .

★ Support, develop potentials, inspire the creative skills, social re-integration, education and advancement of children and teenagers in Africa.

★ Mentorship and training for women and children.; Support children who are victims of exploitation, abuse, armed conflict, psychosocial disorder, broken systems and internally displaced
